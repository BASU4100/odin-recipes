Pizza


Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish made with a soft and chewy yeast-based crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various delicious toppings of your choice. It's a favorite comfort food enjoyed worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Dough:
    • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 packet active dry yeast (7g)
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 3/4 cup warm water
  • Toppings:
    • 1 cup tomato sauce
    • 2 cups mozzarella cheese (shredded)
    • Bell peppers, onions, olives, pepperoni (optional)
    • Fresh basil (optional)
    • Salt, pepper, oregano (to taste)

Steps

  1. Prepare dough by mixing flour, sugar, salt, and yeast. Add warm water and olive oil, knead until smooth. Let it rise for 1 hour.
  2. Preheat the oven to 220Â°C. Roll out the dough into a round base and place on a greased baking tray.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the base, sprinkle mozzarella cheese, and arrange your desired toppings.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and oregano. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese melts.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and serve hot.
